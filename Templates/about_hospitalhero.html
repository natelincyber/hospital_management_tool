About Us



What is HospitalHero?

HospitalHero is versatile platform tailored to COVID-19 that allows doctors to efficiently manage and maintain their hospital. With a clean and simple UI, HospitalHero is easy to use and even easier to manage.

HospitalHero is 100% free

HospitalHero is nothing near a traditional hospital management system. However, this software is a simple and effective alternative so that hospitals in developing countries will not have to worry about the cost of buying and maintaining a professional hospital mangement tool. This is why HospitalHero is free for all.





Our Mission

COVID-19 is a serious virus. Every day, hundreds of people die and thousands more infected. However, there is not enough space in hospitals and clinics to provide aid to infected individuals. We want to help solve this be creating a platform to easily manage rooms and doctors to COVID-19 patients.
